About the Journal

Journal of Literary and Cultural Analysis (JLCA) is an open access and double-blind peer-reviewed International Journal committed to promote original research and findings in the fields like Literature, Culture, Humanities and Social Sciences, etc. It is a multi-disciplinary and Bi-annual academic journal relevant to academicians, researchers, theorists, and scholars interested in Art, Aesthetics, Culture, Literature, Folklore, History, Philosophy, Psychology, Sociology, etc.
